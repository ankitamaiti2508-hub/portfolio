About Me

I combine data
with ground truth.

I'm a Senior Product Manager with 8 years of experience building AI, commerce, and B2B products across FMCG, ecommerce, and financial services.

My approach is simple: dashboards reveal patterns, but customers reveal the reasons behind them. Whether I'm analyzing funnel drop-offs, shadowing sales reps, or sitting in a kirana store testing a voice ordering agent, I believe the best product decisions come from combining data with firsthand observation.

At HCCB and Tata Digital, I've led products that improved adoption, increased revenue, and brought AI into real operational workflows — not as experiments, but as tools people rely on every day.

I enjoy tackling ambiguous problems, aligning cross-functional teams, and turning customer pain points into products that create measurable business impact.

I'm an MBA from ISB Hyderabad and hold a B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering from NIT Allahabad.
